## ⚠️ Read Me First: Are we a good match?

Before you dive into my background, let's save each other some time. Throughout my career, I've learned that alignment on culture, engineering practices, and expectations is far more important than any specific tool in a stack. 

Here is a quick filter to know if we are meant to work together:

### ✅ You might want to hire me if...
- **Your observability bill is out of control:** You are ingesting terabytes of logs, metrics, and traces daily, and you need someone to implement smart sampling, retention policies, and self-hosted alternatives (Prometheus/Grafana/OpenSearch) to optimize costs without losing visibility.
- **Your infrastructure is still manual:** You want to transition from clickOps and manual scripts to a fully declarative, GitOps-driven architecture using Terraform, Helm, and ArgoCD.
- **You are drowning in noise:** Your teams suffer from alert fatigue. You need to shift to an SLO/SLI-driven model where alerts are actionable, based on real user impact, and tied to error budgets.
- **You want to treat Platform as a Product:** You don't just want a "sysadmin" to manage tickets; you want an engineering leader who builds self-service tooling, improves Developer Experience (DevEx), and bridges the gap between infrastructure and product teams.
- **You value continuous learning:** You want to foster an engineering culture that values feedback loops, pair programming, TDD, and pragmatic systems thinking over isolated silos.

### 🛑 Please, don't hire me if...
- **You view SRE simply as "Tier 3 Support":** I believe SRE is a software engineering discipline applied to infrastructure and operations, not just a reactive firefighting team waiting for pages.
- **You want someone to blindly execute:** I am driven by understanding the "why". If your culture punishes asking about the business value or systems implications of a decision, we won't be a good fit.
- **You prioritize quick hacks over sustainable engineering:** I understand business urgency and time-to-market, but I advocate for long-term reliability and manageable technical debt over continuous band-aids.
- **Command & Control is your management style:** I thrive in transparent, highly collaborative, and psychologically safe environments where incidents are treated as blameless learning opportunities, not witch hunts.

*If the green flags resonate with you and your company, keep reading!* 👇
